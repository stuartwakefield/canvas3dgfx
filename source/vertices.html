We then use the Vertex object to plot out the vertices of the model we are creating. As we are creating a cube we need 8 vertices, one for each corner of the cube. You might be asking: why -1.0 and 1.0 and why not any other number? This is a common convention within 3D graphics modelling, it gives us a normalized model that makes it easy to scale, rotate and translate later, being that the natural centre point (the origin) is 0.0.
